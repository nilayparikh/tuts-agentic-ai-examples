COVERAGE SUMMARY
----------------

Plan: Standard
Monthly Premium: $150
Annual Premium: $1,800

DEDUCTIBLES
-----------

- Standard Plan Deductible: $500 per incident
- Emergency Room Deductible: $250 per visit
- Prescription Drug Deductible: $100 per year

COVERAGE LIMITS
---------------

- Per Incident Maximum: $50,000
- Annual Maximum: $200,000
- Lifetime Maximum: $1,000,000

COVERED SERVICES
----------------

1. Primary Care Visits: Covered after deductible, 80/20 co-insurance
2. Specialist Visits: Covered after deductible, 70/30 co-insurance
3. Emergency Room: Covered after ER deductible, 90/10 co-insurance
4. Prescription Drugs:
   - Generic: $10 co-pay
   - Brand Name: $30 co-pay
   - Specialty: 20% co-insurance after drug deductible
5. Preventive Care: Covered at 100%, no deductible
6. Mental Health: Covered after deductible, 80/20 co-insurance
7. Physical Therapy: Up to 30 visits per year, $25 co-pay per visit

EXCLUSIONS
----------

- Cosmetic procedures (unless medically necessary)
- Experimental treatments not FDA-approved
- Services outside the provider network (except emergencies)
- Pre-existing conditions during the first 90-day waiting period

CLAIMS PROCESS
--------------

1. Submit claims within 90 days of service
2. Include itemized bill and provider information
3. Claims processed within 30 business days
4. Appeals must be filed within 60 days of denial
